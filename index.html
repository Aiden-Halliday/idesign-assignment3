Starburst

Every Starburst is bursting with a different flavour...

Originated in the U.K.

Starburst was originally known as Opal Fruits and was launched in the UK in 1960. The name was changed to Starburst in 1998!

Multiple different ways to eat!

Many different varieties of Starburst have been introduced beyond the original fruit chews, including Mini Fruit Chews, Starburst Gummies, and Duo Gummies!

Limited time flavours!

The main flavours are strawberry, lemon, cherry, and orange. However there have been seasonal and old flavours returning such as strawberry snow (winter), lime (original flavour), spooky berries (halloween), and more!
